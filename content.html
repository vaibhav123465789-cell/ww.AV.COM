Explore the Milky Way

The Milky Way is our home galaxy, a vast spiral of stars, dust, and dark matter stretching over 100,000 light-years across. It's estimated to contain over 100 billion stars, including our Sun.

From Earth, we see it as a glowing band across the night sky. But in reality, we're looking at just a small slice of a cosmic masterpiece.
